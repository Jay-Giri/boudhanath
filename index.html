Boudhanath stupa
10/10/2020

In the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal, stands the magnificent Boudhanath Stupa, a revered symbol of Buddhist spirituality. This UNESCO World Heritage Site, with its iconic watchful Buddha eyes, exudes a profound sense of peace. Its history, while shrouded in legend, dates back centuries, surviving earthquakes and adversity. Pilgrims and visitors alike circumambulate the stupa, spinning prayer wheels, as the vibrant Tibetan community surrounding it offers a glimpse into a rich culture. Boudhanath Stupa is not just a religious site; it's a timeless sanctuary where people come to find solace, share their beliefs, and connect with the essence of humanity's spiritual quest.

Shey Phoksundo
5/8/2020

Tucked away in the remote Dolpo region of Nepal lies the breathtaking Shey Phoksundo Lake. Nestled amidst towering mountains and rugged terrain, this pristine alpine lake captivates with its crystal-clear turquoise waters, reflecting the stark beauty of its surroundings. It's not just a geographical wonder but also a cultural treasure, surrounded by Tibetan Buddhist villages that have retained their traditions for centuries. Trekkers and adventurers are drawn to its serene shores, making it a destination for those seeking both natural beauty and cultural immersion in the heart of Nepal's wilderness.

Annapurna trekking Trail
10/7/2020

The Annapurna Trekking Trail is a trekker's dream, weaving through the majestic Annapurna mountain range in Nepal. This iconic trail offers a kaleidoscope of landscapes, from lush rhododendron forests to high-altitude deserts, all framed by the towering peaks of Annapurna and Machapuchare. Along the way, trekkers encounter welcoming villages, diverse cultures, and warm-hearted locals who offer a glimpse into traditional Himalayan life. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or a novice hiker, the Annapurna Trekking Trail offers a memorable journey through one of the world's most spectacular and accessible trekking destinations.
